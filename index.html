Mathematical Formulas

Coulomb's Law (Electrostatic Force)

F = k × (q₁ × q₂) / r²
k = Coulomb's constant (8.99×10⁹ N⋅m²/C²)
q₁, q₂ = charges of particles
r = distance between particles

Newton's Second Law

F = m × a
F = force vector
m = mass of particle
a = acceleration

Velocity Update

v = v₀ + a × Δt
v = new velocity
v₀ = initial velocity
Δt = time step

Position Update

x = x₀ + v × Δt
x = new position
x₀ = initial position
v = velocity

Kinetic Energy

KE = ½ × m × v²
KE = kinetic energy
m = mass
v = velocity magnitude

Potential Energy

PE = k × (q₁ × q₂) / r
PE = potential energy between two charges
